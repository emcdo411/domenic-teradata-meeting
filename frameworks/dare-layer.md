## Overview

DARE is a four-step system used to:

- Strip narrative
- Expose structural gaps
- Reframe risk
- Establish entry points

---

## D — Define

Identify what is actually present.

Remove narrative, positioning, and abstraction.

**Output:** Reality baseline

---

## A — Assess

Measure the gap between:

- Current state
- Required state

Anchored in regulatory, academic, and market signals.

**Output:** Misalignment clarity

---

## R — Reframe

Convert the gap into a new understanding.

Shift from:
- Tooling → accountability
- Capability → responsibility

**Output:** Strategic narrative

---

## E — Execute

Use the reframed gap as an entry point.

Define:
- Where to enter
- What to anchor
- How to convert

**Output:** Engagement pathway
