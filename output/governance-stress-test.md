## Executive Summary

Teradata is attempting a structural transition from legacy data warehouse provider to autonomous AI platform. The company’s advantage is embedded enterprise data gravity. The risk is governance incompleteness at the accountability layer.

The critical gap is not technical capability. The gap is the absence of a **named operations sponsor responsible for post-deployment decision accountability**.

This gap becomes material under emerging regulatory regimes and competitive positioning pressure from Snowflake and Databricks.
